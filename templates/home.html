Welcome to YouRank

Welcome to YouRank! We are glad that you chose to take part in our experiment. From here you can either continue an evaluation or start a new one. Also, we provide some details about what will be required from you as an user.

About the Experiment

We ask that each user evaluates 20 pairs of YouTube videos. For each pair, we want to know which video of the pair you would share (in the send the link sense) with a friend or a group of friends. Think of the action of sharing content in Online Social Networks such as Facebook, Google+ or even email.

We also ask of you to provide additional feedback on each video pair if you think wish to provide any. This is an optional step but you can use it to tell us that you don't really care about any of the two videos in the pair. You can also use it to provide feedback or state that you found the evaluation of that pair confusing.

Starting/Continuing Experiments

Please use the New Experiment to start your evaluation. You will be provided with a unique identifier which we that you write down in case you need to pause the evaluation and continue later. In the the Continue Experiment link you can use your unique id to pick up where you left off.
