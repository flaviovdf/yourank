Welcome to YouRank! We are glad that you chose to take part in our experiment. Here we provide some details about what will be required from you as an user. Also, from here you can either continue an evaluation or start a new one.

About the experiment

We ask that each user evaluates $num_pairs pairs of YouTube videos. For each pair, we would like to know:

  1. Which video did you enjoy watching the most?
  2. Which video you would be most willing to share with a friend or group of friends, that is, which of the two videos you most probably would send the link to a friend?
  3. Which video do you think will become more popular over time on YouTube?

Although the three questions are similar, each one captures a different perception of the video pair. The first one is related to your individual taste, we want to know which video did you most enjoy watching. The second one wants to know which would you share with friends, for example, you may prefer a video but find another more interesting to share with your friends due to it being funnier. The last question is interested in your perception of YouTube as a whole, that is, we want to know what you think will be more popular regardless of your tastes or sharing habits.

We also ask you to provide additional feedback on each pair of videos if you think wish to provide any. This is an optional step but you can use it to provide feedback about the pair or state that you found the evaluation of that pair confusing.

We request that you refrain from visiting the video page on YouTube. This may lead to bias in our research

Starting/Continuing evaluations

Please use the New Evaluation to start your evaluation. You will be provided with a unique identifier which we request of you write it down in case you need to pause the evaluation and continue later. In the the Continue Evaluation link you can use your unique id to pick up where you left off.
